Iron Condor — Strategy Dashboard

A high-performance "Pro Terminal" for professional options traders, designed to track multi-leg strategies on the NIFTY50 index with real-time risk visualization and automated Greek monitoring.

Project Overview

The Iron Condor Dashboard bridges the gap between raw financial data and actionable insights. It simplifies complex multi-leg strategy management by providing a centralized view of Total Net MTM, Portfolio Delta, and interactive Risk Zones. Built for sub-second precision, it ensures traders can manage directional exposure and break-even points with confidence.

System Workflow

1

Data Generation/Ingestion

The backend pulls live market data or generates high-fidelity simulated snapshots including Greeks and Spot Price movements.

2

WebSocket Stream

A persistent bidirectional connection handles sub-second data broadcasts, ensuring the UI stays updated without refreshes.

3

Real-time Processing

The backend recalculates portfolio state while the frontend parses JSON payloads to update MTM counters and re-render Risk Bars.

4

Risk Monitoring & Alerts

The system continuously checks price proximity to "Short" or "Hedge" legs, triggering visual Amber/Red alerts if danger zones are entered.

5

State Persistence

Configurations and historical trade snapshots are maintained in structured JSON, ensuring session continuity and data integrity.

Key Features

📏

Dynamic Risk Bars

Visual representation of "Profit Zones" vs "Risk Zones" based on custom strike distances and hedge legs.

🧭

Portfolio Delta Meter

Aggregates directional risk across all active legs for instant delta-neutral management feedback.

🚨

Automated Alerts

Threshold-based notifications and visual cues for "Near Strike" warnings and MTM milestone achievements.

📊

Expiry Breakdown

Smart categorization of MTM, Credit, and Risk profiles based on specific option contract expiry dates.

Primary Use Cases

Professional traders use this terminal for Live Strategy Tracking of complex spreads, Risk Mitigation through break-even proximity monitoring, and Portfolio Balancing to maintain delta-neutral positions in volatile markets.
