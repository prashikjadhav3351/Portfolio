Heardu Website

A powerful platform designed specifically for community management, feedback parsing, and providing administrators actionable intelligence.

Project Overview

Developed using FastAPI and PostgreSQL, this system efficiently connects users, letting them log feedback seamlessly. It serves static templates compiled with Jinja2 while maintaining robust REST APIs. The goal was superior performance and quick response times under load.
